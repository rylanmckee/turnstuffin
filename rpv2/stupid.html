Our Mission

The Habit Burger Grill offers some of the best burgers money can buy. Our history goes way back to 1969, where the first Habit Grill opened in Santa Barabara, CA. We like to think it's no coincidence that man first walked on the moon soon after the Habit was established. Our goal is to serve our customers only the freshest ingedients, never frozen and cooked right where you can see it.
